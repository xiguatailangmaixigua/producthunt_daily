What is LinktoDM?

Its a powerful Instagram automation tool that helps you:

  • Auto-send DMs to users who comment on your posts/reels

  • Build Chat Automation flows using a simple conversational format no complicated logic nodes like other tools!

  • Grow your followers with our unique Ask to Follow feature

  • Instantly reply to story reactions

  • Use AI-powered replies for comments and messages

  • Track performance with a smart analytics dashboard


Built exclusively for Instagram, LinktoDM helps brands, creators, and marketers save time, increase engagement, and turn comments into meaningful conversations.
